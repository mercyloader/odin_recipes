Butter Beef

This butter beef is so tender, you can cut it with a spoon. "This is an easy busy-day favorite," says recipe reviewer Laura W. "I love to come home and only have to make some potatoes or rice and a salad!"

This butter beef recipe is easy to make in your slow cooker for melt-in-your mouth, buttery beef with tons of flavor. Serve over cooked egg noodles.

This irresistibly rich butter-based beef recipe uses just three ingredients to create a luscious slow cooker meal. Stew meat, onion soup mix, and butter combine for an intensely savory bite you'll want to experience over and over again. Add this popular beef recipe to your rotation for a delightfully meaty dinner any day of the week.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds cubed beef stew meat
  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 (1-ounce) envelope dry onion soup mix

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place beef and butter in a slow cooker. Sprinkle onion soup mix over top.
  3. Cover, and cook until meat is tender, on Low for 8 hours, or on High for 4 to 5 hours. Stir once or twice during cook time.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
